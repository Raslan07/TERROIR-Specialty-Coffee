From seed to cup

The Process

We believe in transparency. Every step, from green bean to roasted coffee, happens here, by hand, with intention.

01

Sourcing

We travel to origin, cup hundreds of samples, and build relationships with farmers who share our values. Each lot is selected for its unique character and traceability.

02

Green Storage

Beans rest in climate-controlled conditions, preserving their potential. We roast within six months of harvest to capture peak flavor.

03

Roasting

Small batches, precise heat, careful attention. We develop profiles that highlight each origin's unique qualities—never over-roasting, never masking defects.

04

Resting

Freshly roasted coffee needs time. We let our beans rest for at least 48 hours before packaging, allowing flavors to settle and develop.

05

Brewing

The final step is yours. We provide detailed brewing guides for each coffee, helping you bring out its best. Pour over, French press, espresso—the choice is yours.
